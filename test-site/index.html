The board has 112 squares : 10 lines x 11 columns with one "citadel" square in both sides. Each player has 28 pieces:

  • 1 Shah
  • 1 Minister (Ferz)
  • 1 Vizir(Wazîr)
  • 2 Giraffes (Zurâfa)
  • 2 Pickets (Talî'a)
  • 2 Horses
  • 2 Camel
  • 2 Rooks ("Rukhs")
  • 2 War Machines ("Dabbâbas")
  • 2 Camels (Jamal)
  • 2 Elephants
  • 11 Pawns, each of which promotes to the major piece it represents

Tamerlane Chess is an old Persian Chess Variant. There are several ways for an opening setup to be arranged. To learn more visit Tamerlane Chess History A common one is as follows:

  • White's side (bottom row, from the left): elephant, (space), camel, (space), war machine, (space), war machine, (space), camel, (space), elephant.
  • Second row (from the left): rook, knight, picket, giraffe, general, king, vizir, giraffe, picket, knight, rook.
  • Third row (from the left): pawn of pawns, pawn of war engines, pawn of camels, pawn of elephants, pawn of generals, pawn of kings, pawn of vizirs, pawn of giraffes, pawn of pickets, pawn of knights, pawn of rooks.
